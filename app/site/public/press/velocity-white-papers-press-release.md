# NEWS RELEASE

**The Velocity White Papers**
Government of Alberta · Ministry of Technology and Innovation

The Ministry of Technology and Innovation is releasing a series of technical white papers which explain Alberta's recent efforts in using artificial intelligence to modernize our government technologies and keep our systems cyber safe. This collection, known as The Velocity White Papers, is available at thevelocitywhitepapers.com and is shared as an open resources to support Alberta government staff, public bodies, municipalities, and is shared openly with provinces, territories, and governments across Canada and around the world.

Alberta is already recognized as a leader in artificial intelligence. These papers present a blueprint for others to emulate our successes. 

**Minister of Technology and Innovation, Nate Glubish:**

> "It is critical for governments across Canada to leverage these technologies to modernize and keep services secure. We are facing an unprecedented amount of risk in cybersecurity, and these tools help provide safe and effective government services that protect privacy while saving real money. Alberta has already saved more than fifty million dollars in the last year using these technologies, and we expect even greater savings to come in the months ahead."

The Velocity White Papers expand Alberta's open information on AI enablement, building on the Alberta AI Academy, which has educated thousands of public servants on the safe and effective use of artificial intelligence. The Academy has been accessed by more than ten thousand Albertans and Canadians to help public bodies better leverage and understand AI technologies.

For more information, visit thevelocitywhitepapers.com or follow Minister Glubish's Substack at https://nateglubish.substack.com/.
